the designing, testing and building of machines, structures and processes using maths and science.Studying it can lead to a rewarding career. Engineering is a discipline dedicated to problem solving.

Engineering is an exciting subject because it combines scientific and mathematical knowledge with creative thinking. It teaches you to question the established ways of doing things and to come up with new ideas. In order to do this, you need creativity and an ability to look beyond what has been done before.

An Engineer , or Senior Engineer, uses math and science to solve different technical problems. Their main duties include developing new products for companies or individuals to use, providing maintenance to current products to enhance use and designing new machines to improve an organization's efficiencies
